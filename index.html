We all go through an egg-sistential crisis once a while...

University can be a very difficult time for everyone. New way of life, new people.


It is always good to know the available resources that you have to help yourself and others.


Just know, you are not alone!




Did you know that 1 in 5 University students has experienced at least one common mental health problem?


The most common ones are:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
